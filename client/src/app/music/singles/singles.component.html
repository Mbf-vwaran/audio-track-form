Let’s upload your track’s cover

Your album's cover must be a square .jpg, .jpeg, or .png file with a minimum resolution of 3000 x 3000 pixels. Please ensure that the image is not blurry or pixelated and does not exceed 10 MB in size.

You cannot have social media logos or handles, website links, or brand/record label logos on the cover.

If you want to include some text, please limit it to the artist's name and/or the release name.

The music stores set these rules, and we must follow them. So, if your album cover does not follow the above guidelines, we will have to reject your release.
